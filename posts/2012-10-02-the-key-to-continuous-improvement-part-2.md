- Review the work (5 min)
  - What was completed?
  - What was worked on?
  - What came in out of band?
  - What didn't the PM know about?
- Issues (15 min)
  - Post-its: Great, Bad, Idea, Puzzling
  - Group them and vote
- Talk (30 min)
  - Talk about them in rank
- A / M / L / A (5 min)
  - Awesome
  - Mystery
  - Lesson
  - Actions
