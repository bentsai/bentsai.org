Cleaning up a computer

- Run CCleaner.
- Disconnect Google accounts from Chrome.
- Remove cookies from Chrome (for some reason, CCleaner didn't do this completely).
- Delete bookmarks.
- Deauthorize iTunes.
- Run CCleaner to wipe free space.
